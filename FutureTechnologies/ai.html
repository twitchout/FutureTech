AI: A Brief Summary

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a multidisciplinary field that aims to create machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding natural language, and perceiving the environment. AI systems are built upon algorithms and mathematical models that enable them to process vast amounts of data, recognize patterns, and make decisions with minimal human intervention.
